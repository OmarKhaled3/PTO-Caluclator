How This Works
Past & future time off
Dates after the as-of date store future approved leave. Only dates within the selected year can be chosen.
As-of-date logic
The main PTO balance counts accrual and leave usage up to the selected date only.
Casual leave
Tracked against a 7-day yearly allowance and deducted from PTO balance simultaneously.
Entry types
Each date stores a leave type (annual/casual) and an amount (full/half). Dates outside the as-of year cannot be selected.
